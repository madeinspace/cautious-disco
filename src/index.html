Why we exist?

The solution:

Time consuming and expensive process. Due to intermediaries involved in every step and the time spent waiting for funds to transfer, permits to be granted and paperwork to be drawn, property acquisition is a lengthy and inefficient process.

With REALinvest, anyone from anywhere is able to buy Real Estate through our listing market with an option to pay with either solely by cryptocurrencies or a combination of cryptocurrency and fiat currency. With Ethereum acting as a base platform and its ability to execute smart contracts, the Real Estate industry will be the most to benefit. Eliminating the intermediates, ability to perform global transactions almost instantly and cross-border transactions is the Real Estate of the future.
